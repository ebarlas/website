Summary

I'm a generalist software engineer specializing in backend Java web applications deployed in AWS Cloud with an emphasis on customer identity, authentication, and administration. I'm a thoughtful and deliberate developer seeking simple and scalable solutions to problems. I have a record of stewarding and evolving software systems over the course of many years while maintaining high quality code and employing modern techniques. I am responsible for the full spectrum of software lifecycle stages, from specification and design to deployment, monitoring, maintenance, and database administration. I empower my colleagues by providing tools, libraries, and frameworks and by cultivating an atmosphere of growth, learning, and collaboration. I enjoy a range of programming activities including C++ game programming, Raspberry Pi actuator programming, mobile development, and server development. I also enjoy teaching computer programming topics to newcomers as a volunteer at the local CoderDojo chapter and at Santa Rosa Junior College.
